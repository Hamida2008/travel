What’s included

Taking pride of place in Sydney’s commercial, arts and cultural hub, the Capella Sydney is an enviable new luxury offering in the heart of the harbour city’s lively CBD. Housed in an intricately restored heritage-listed building that was initially developed in the early 1930’s, this beautiful hotel balances storied architectural features with a light-palleted contemporary design aesthetic. Capella Sydney has been designed to indulge guests with luxurious accommodation, world-class cuisine, and cultural immersion.

Staying in a heritage homestead dating from the 1850’s, guests enjoy a taste of classic Australian country hospitality while a few ‘Wild Bush Luxury’ touches ensure comfort, style and a truly memorable outback experience. With just five guest rooms, each with en-suite bathroom and air conditioning and designed in an early settler/pioneer style, Arkaba has made the transformation from outback sheep station to private wildlife conservancy and offers guests a broader wilderness experience.

World-renowned luxury on the wildly beautiful south coast of Kangaroo Island is available at this iconic cliffside lodge. Recently rebuilt with the features that guests loved best along with some considerable extras, there is so much to enjoy and explore at the new-look Southern Ocean Lodge.

Spread across 120 rooms and suites, each stay at Eos promises stunning views across the city, towards the scenic Adelaide Hills, or overlooking the banks of the River Torrens. Innovative and intuitive technologies are merged into each room, including keyless entry and an in-room tablet where guests can order in-room dining, make restaurant reservations, and more. Other touches include a complimentary minibar with curated South Australian products and luxury bathroom amenities.

Set on the northernmost tip of the Great Barrier Reef, Lizard Island is a leading luxury lodge experience in one of the world’s most renowned natural environments. Nestled within a sprawling national park enriched with diverse Australian fauna and flora, with direct access to the world’s largest coral reef system and home to only 40 exclusive rooms and suites, including the acclaimed House at Lizard Island, this island retreat promises an immersive escape.
