What’s included

Taking pride of place in Sydney’s commercial, arts and cultural hub, the Capella Sydney is an enviable new luxury offering in the heart of the harbour city’s lively CBD. Housed in an intricately restored heritage-listed building that was initially developed in the early 1930’s, this beautiful hotel balances storied architectural features with a light-palleted contemporary design aesthetic. Capella Sydney has been designed to indulge guests with luxurious accommodation, world-class cuisine, and cultural immersion.

World-renowned luxury on the wildly beautiful south coast of Kangaroo Island is available at this iconic cliffside lodge. Recently rebuilt with the features that guests loved best along with some considerable extras, there is so much to enjoy and explore at the new-look Southern Ocean Lodge.

An elegant sanctuary nestled alongside the historic Mount Lofty House, Sequoia is a luxury lodge boasting panoramic views of the Adelaide Hills, personalised service, and exceptional food and wine. It is named for the 150-year-old Sequoia trees planted by Arthur and Martha Hardy, the original owners of the adjacent Mount Lofty House.

Nestled atop rust-red dunes, Longitude 131° overlooks Australia’s best-known natural icon, Uluru, and is arguably one of the best luxury wilderness camps in the world. The welcome open spaces of the central Dune House include the restaurant with ever-changing views of the mysterious monolith Uluru as a backdrop for every meal, as well as lounging areas in contemporary campaign style, ideal for relaxing with an icy drink and exchanging stories of the day’s outback adventures.

Finniss River Lodge is an exciting and intimate experiential luxury lodge built alongside the vast coastal floodplains of the Top End. Owned and operated by third-generation pastoralists, this remarkable property offers a profound sense of isolation within a diversity of stunning landscapes, all while being easily accessible from Darwin.

Found within the heart of the World Heritage-listed Daintree Rainforest alongside the Mossman River, Silky Oaks Lodge delivers a five-star luxury lodge experience in Tropical North Queensland. This premium escape is perfectly positioned between Tropical North Queensland’s natural attractions of the primordial Daintree Rainforest and the sparkling Great Barrier Reef. Only an hour from Cairns and a 20-minute drive from Port Douglas, Silky Oaks Lodge offers a secluded escape into one of Australia’s most magnificent landscapes.

The House at Lizard Island is a world-class reef house, perfectly positioned on the doorstep of the Great Barrier Reef. Embodying all things authentically Australian while offering unparalleled privacy and personalised service, The House offers barefoot adventure, a profound connection to nature, and laidback luxury at its finest.
